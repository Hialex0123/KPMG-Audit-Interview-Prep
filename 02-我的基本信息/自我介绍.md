## 🇨🇳 中文版（约1分20秒）

各位面试官好，我叫黄璐尧，是武汉大学金融学专业的26届应届毕业生，很高兴今天有机会来毕马威参加面试。

实习方面，我有三段经历。最主要的一段是在广州金融科技股份有限公司，通过舆情监控和企业财报分析对辖区企业进行风险监测，在找到完整证据链后，为当地政府撰写风险提示报告。
这段经历和外部审计的底层逻辑相似，也让我对审计产生了更明确的职业兴趣。另外两段分别在江西省发改委和太平养老保险，以宏观政策研究和公文写作为主，锻炼了我的书面表达能力。

技能方面，我熟练使用Python和R进行数据分析，近期也在系统学习AI Agent，能够自主搭建自动化工作流，目前已经实现AI拟人写作以及自动化数据爬取项目——这和毕马威推进智能审计的方向高度契合。英语方面雅思7分，日常读写和沟通没有障碍。

在校期间，我担任武汉大学天文爱好者协会会长，管理超过两千名社员，统筹协调华中地区多场大型活动，在此过程中培养了在高压环境下统筹全局、带领团队的能力。

我选择毕马威，是因为它在四大中对智能审计的投入最为系统，我希望把自己的数据能力真正融入审计工作中。期待能加入团队，谢谢。

## 🇬🇧 English Version（约1分30秒）

Hi, good morning. I'm Huang Luyao, a Finance student at Wuhan University, Class of 2026.

I've had three internships. The most relevant was at Guangzhou Financial Technology, where I monitored business risks for local government clients. I tracked companies through public sentiment data and financial reports — once I built a solid chain of evidence, I wrote risk alert reports for the government. That process is very similar to what auditors do, and it's what got me interested in audit. My other two internships, at a government planning commission and an insurance company, were mainly about policy research and document writing.

On the skills side, I use Python and R for data analysis, and I've been building AI agent workflows — including projects for automated report writing and web scraping. My IELTS is 7.0, so English is no problem.

At university, I was President of the Astronomy Association, leading over 2,000 members and organizing large events across Central China. That taught me how to manage a team and stay calm under pressure.

I chose KPMG because it's the most committed to technology-driven audit among the Big Four. I want to be somewhere my data skills matter from day one. Thank you.
